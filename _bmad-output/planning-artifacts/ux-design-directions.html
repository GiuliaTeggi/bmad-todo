Direction AMinimal Light — recommended baseline. Aligns with emotional goals (calm, focused). Hover-reveal delete. Indigo accent.
Direction BCard-Based — slightly elevated surface. Rounder, softer feel. Good alternative if a more "app-like" frame is preferred.
Direction CDark Pro — developer terminal aesthetic. High personality but may conflict with "calm" goal. Best if dark mode is a priority.
Direction DWarm / Friendly — orange accent, pill input, circular checkboxes. Friendly and approachable. Less conservative.
Direction EHigh Contrast — maximum accessibility. Explicit "Delete" labels, always-visible actions. Functional over aesthetic.
Direction FSpacious Editorial — underline input, generous whitespace, lowercase branding. Refined and distinctive but unconventional.
